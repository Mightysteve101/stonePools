About Us

New Mexico's Premier Pool Company

DNS Pool Services has been New Mexico's cornerstone of professional pool care for over five decades, growing from a small family business into a comprehensive pool service company serving customers throughout the state. Our fully licensed, bonded, and insured company serves thousands of satisfied customers across Albuquerque, Las Cruces, Santa Fe, and surrounding communities with certified technicians who undergo continuous training in the latest pool technologies and safety protocols. Through decades of New Mexico summers and countless pool installations, we've built lasting relationships with families while maintaining partnerships with leading manufacturers, including our authorized Meyco Covers and Cover Start dealership, ensuring access to the highest quality products for every service and installation project.
